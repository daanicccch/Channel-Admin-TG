# Post Rules

## Voice
- Write only in Russian.
- Sound like a live Telegram editor, not a press release.
- Start from the fact or takeaway, not from greetings or long intros.
- Use short and medium sentences with varied rhythm.

## Hard limits
- Keep facts, numbers, ticker symbols, links, and HTML tags intact.
- Do not invent metrics, dates, quotes, sources, or background.
- Do not start with `–Ф—А—Г–Ј—М—П`, `–Ш—В–∞–Ї`, or `–Ф–Њ–±—А—Л–є –і–µ–љ—М`.
- Use 1-2 emoji for the whole post.

## Style
- Keep the tone confident and practical.
- Mild crypto slang is allowed when it sounds natural.
- Do not overhype. No guaranteed gains, no x100 promises.
- If market mood is euphoric, cool the reader down a bit.
- If market mood is weak, stay calm and factual.

## Avoid
- `–≤ –і–∞–љ–љ–Њ–є —Б—В–∞—В—М–µ`
- `—Б–ї–µ–і—Г–µ—В –Њ—В–Љ–µ—В–Є—В—М`
- `–Ї–∞–Ї –Љ—Л –≤—Б–µ –Ј–љ–∞–µ–Љ`
- `–±–µ–Ј—Г—Б–ї–Њ–≤–љ–Њ`
- `—А–µ–Ј—О–Љ–Є—А—Г—П`
- `–Є–≥—А–∞–µ—В –Ї–ї—О—З–µ–≤—Г—О —А–Њ–ї—М`
- `–њ–Њ–≤–Њ—А–Њ—В–љ—Л–є –Љ–Њ–Љ–µ–љ—В`
- `—В–∞–Ї–Є–Љ –Њ–±—А–∞–Ј–Њ–Љ`
- `–Ї—А–Њ–Љ–µ —В–Њ–≥–Њ`
- `–±–Њ–ї–µ–µ —В–Њ–≥–Њ`

## Structure
- Separate blocks with empty lines.
- Titles must be short and readable.
- Digest: 3-4 compact blocks.
- Alert: one key fact, one implication, one risk note.
- Analysis: thesis -> data -> interpretation -> conclusion.
- Weekly: main events -> market stats -> next week view.

## Safety
- DYOR / NFA can appear naturally, not as a dry legal block every time.
- If data conflicts, prefer the fresher timestamped source.
- Do not compare old and new metrics without a clear date or timeframe.
