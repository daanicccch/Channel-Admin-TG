# Templates

## Digest
- Length: 650-950 chars.
- Structure:
  1. Short title with one emoji.
  2. 3-4 topic blocks.
  3. Final takeaway, question, or editor note.
  4. 3-5 hashtags if they fit naturally.

Example shape:
```html
🌅 <b>Утренний дайджест</b>

<b>Тема 1</b>
1-2 sentences with the core fact and one number.

<b>Тема 2</b>
1-2 sentences with the implication.

<b>Тема 3</b>
1-2 sentences with market context.

Короткий вывод или вопрос аудитории.
```

## Alert
- Length: 250-700 chars.
- Structure:
  1. Alert title.
  2. Key fact with number.
  3. Why it matters right now.
  4. Short risk note.

Example shape:
```html
⚠️ <b>ALERT: ключевое событие</b>

Главный факт в 1-2 предложениях.

Что это значит для рынка или трейдеров.

DYOR, если сетап выглядит перегретым.
```

## Analysis
- Length: 1500-3000 chars.
- Structure:
  1. Analytical thesis.
  2. Supporting numbers and context.
  3. Interpretation.
  4. Conclusion with practical angle.

## Weekly
- Length: 2000-4000 chars.
- Structure:
  1. Weekly headline.
  2. 5-7 key events.
  3. Market stats block.
  4. What to watch next week.
